Our Story

1993

We opened for business

Initial focus was providing signs to meet the ADA which was enacted in 1992. Products offered quickly expanded to meet client requirements. Initially, this was done using outside manufacturers and vendors. Our objective was to bring manufacturing in-house and we focused our efforts to do so.

1999

Move to Jefferson Street

Working from our shop in Pottstown,PA. Expansion of tools, equipment and manpower and we were on our way. Continued growth created demand for more space.

2003

Third Location

Relocated 4 blocks south in Pottstown. The building was now ready for expansion. Full service metal and weld fabrication, CNC machines and lasers now increasing our productivity, quality and maintaining production schedules. Space was needed and we moved North approximately 4 miles.

2010

Fourth Location

Initial focus was providing signs to meet the ADA which was enacted in 1992. Products offered quickly expanded to meet client requirements. Initially, this was done using outside manufacturers and vendors. Our objective was to bring manufacturing in-house and we focused our efforts to do so.

Today

48 S Reading Ave., Boyertown, PA

We provide complete sign services from concept to completion and welcome the opportunity to put our team to work for any of your sign requirements. We also provide LEED credits with use of recycled content and local manufacturing. In-house we recycle all our paper, glass and metal to minimize our impact on the planet.
