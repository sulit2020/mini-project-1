Wayfinding

Wayfinding signs are very effective solutions for directing people where to go and enhancing your campus. They come in a variety of shapes and colors. Traditionally, post and panel signs are not internally lit, but have a separate source of light shining on it, typically from ground. Vinyl lettering is most common, but dimensional lettering is also available.
